Interface

In 25 years a number of organisations have each their own colony on mars. The projects are still in their infancy and are reliant on their parent organisations for materials to survive.

Some time later world war 3 breaks out and the colonies are left to fend for themselves. They each have a speciliazation where they can convert one element into another with a gain, but only one, so they have to cooporate by trading to survive.

  • A: Map window where you can see the other colonies, mouse over will show their situation and clicking on them will select them in the transfer-window
  • B: Chat window where you can write to the other colonies. Everyone can see the all-chatroom and with the tabs you can write privatly to other colonies. Your name will be highlighted.
  • C: Score window shows how many points you have right now, this will go up or down depending on your situation
  • D: Trade window to transfer materials to other colonies. First field is receiving colony, second is material and the third is amount
  • E: Production window, second field is amount and there is a productiontime of 10 seconds
  • F: Inventory window, shows how many of each materials you have left in your colonies. Clicking a row will select that material in the transfer window.
  • G: Log of transfers and other game events. Your name will be highlighted
  • H: Time window shows how many seconds that are left of this round
